tips for successful job application

How to write a good CV?

- Keep it as short as possible, maximum 2 pages.
- Make sure you do not make a typo error in your e-mail address.
- Always state the country code in your phone number.
- Indicate the information regarding your Legal Status in the targeted country (e.g. Work permit if you are not a EU citizen).
- Always put the start and end date of your previous employments.
- Start the list of your previous employments with the most recent one.
- State the name of the company, the position that you had and the tasks you had to do in bullet points.

How to prepare for an interview?

- Read the job description carefully; do some research about the position, if possible, before going to the interview.
- Come up with questions in case something is not clear.
- Get some information about the company; check out its website, read some articles about it, etc. Imagine yourself working there.
- Think about your motivation and why you want the position.
- Be honest, do not exaggerate!
- Dress according to the occasion.
- Do not worry, we want you to succeed as well!

After joining

We find it important to support the onboarding process and manage the integration of our new colleagues. To achieve work satisfaction we provide essential information after the joining through: - 2-day Instructor-led Induction Program where you have the chance to meet the members of the Leadership Team of TCS and interact with them, understand our business and values that drive our everyday work - Project-trainings ensure that you learn how to perform the tasks in your role - New Joiner Integration SPOCs (Single Point of Contact) help you find your way in the organization - Through our Onboarding Survey we want to know your opinion about your experience

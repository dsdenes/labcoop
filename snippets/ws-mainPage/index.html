life science
- how do we work?
Life Sciences and Health Care Tower in TCS Hungary deals with pharmacovigilance and publishing activity for global pharma companies. We serve Patient Safety, with the core purpose to add value to our Client's products and safeguard the success of the business by focusing on protection of patients. It is ensured that all relevant information concerning the safety of the Client's products is collected, evaluated and communicated. Timely and efficient reporting of adverse events and other information pertinent to the safety profile of products to regulatory authorities around the world is essential to comply with current regulations and guidelines. We are involved in publishing activities that is mostly connected to regulatory part of the pharmaceutical industry - but also makes sure that drugs of the Client can be used safely all over the word. Besides being associates at TCS we can feel that we are also active and important parts at our Clients' everyday life and production - the business results show that they trust our knowledge and expertise.
